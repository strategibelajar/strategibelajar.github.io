In a basketball game:

1. There are typically two teams playing against each other.

2. Each team typically has five players on the court at a time.

3. The equipment used in a basketball game includes a basketball, which is a spherical ball, and a hoop with a net attached to it. The court itself is also a crucial piece of equipment, with specific markings and dimensions.

4. Players score points by shooting the basketball through the opponent's hoop. A field goal made from outside the three-point line is worth three points, while a field goal made from inside the three-point line is worth two points. Additionally, players can score one point by making a free throw, which is awarded after certain fouls. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
