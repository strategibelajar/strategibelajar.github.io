14. It takes 6 volunteers a total of 1hour and 30minutes to prepare food for 1200 homeless people. Assuming they work at the same rate, how many hours would it take 30 volunteers to prepare food for 13200 homeless people?


Jawaban:

To solve this problem, we can start by finding the rate at which the 6 volunteers work. We know that it takes them 1 hour and 30 minutes to prepare food for 1200 homeless people. Converting the time to minutes would give us 90 minutes.

Rate of work for 6 volunteers = 1200 / 90 = 13.33 (people per minute)

Now, let's find out how many minutes it would take 30 volunteers to prepare food for 13200 homeless people.

Rate of work for 30 volunteers = (Rate of work for 6 volunteers) * (number of volunteers)

Rate of work for 30 volunteers = 13.33 * 30 = 399.9 (people per minute)

To find the time it takes, we divide the number of people by the rate.

Time = 13200 / 399.9 = 33.01 minutes

Therefore, it would take approximately 33.01 minutes for 30 volunteers to prepare food for 13200 homeless people.

Preparing for hibernation

32. It takes 6 volunteers a total of 1hour and 30minutes to prepare food for 1200 homeless people. Assuming they work at the same rate, how many hours would it take 30 volunteers to prepare food for 13200 homeless people?


Let's break down the problem step by step:

1. 6 volunteers can prepare food for 1200 homeless people in 1 hour and 30 minutes. First, let's convert this time to minutes:
1 hour = 60 minutes
1 hour 30 minutes = 60 minutes + 30 minutes = 90 minutes

2. So, 6 volunteers can prepare food for 1200 homeless people in 90 minutes.

3. To find out how many people 1 volunteer can prepare food for in 90 minutes, we divide 1200 by 6:
1200 / 6 = 200

4. Now, we know that 1 volunteer can prepare food for 200 people in 90 minutes.

5. To find out how many people 1 volunteer can prepare food for in 1 minute, we divide 200 by 90:
200 / 90 ≈ 2.22 (rounded to 2 decimal places)

6. So, 1 volunteer can prepare food for approximately 2.22 people in 1 minute.

7. Now, we want to know how many hours it would take 30 volunteers to prepare food for 13,200 homeless people. Let's set up a proportion:
(30 volunteers) / (1 volunteer) = (13,200 people) / (x hours)

8. To find 'x' (the number of hours), we can cross-multiply and solve for 'x':
30 * x = 1 * 13,200

9. Divide both sides by 30 to solve for 'x':
x = 13,200 / 30

10. Calculate 'x':
x = 440

So, it would take 30 volunteers approximately 440 hours to prepare food for 13,200 homeless people if they work at the same rate as the initial scenario.
