Penjelasan:

⁕ Future perfect tense, digunakan ketika menunjukkan suatu aksi yang telah selesai di masa depan. Polanya adalah:

(+) S + Will + Have + V3

(-) S + Will + not + Have + V3

(?) Will + S + Have + V3 + ...?

⁕ Past perfect tense, digunakan ketika menunjukkan suatu aksi yang telah selesai sebelum aksi lainnya terjadi di masa lalu.

(+) S + Had + V3

(-) S + Had + not + V3

(?) Had + S +  V3 + ...?

⁕ Present perfect tense, digunakan ketika menunjukkan suatu aksi yang dimulai di masa lalu dan tetap berpengaruh hingga sekarang.

(+) S + Have/Has + V3

(-) S + Have/Has + not + V3

(?) Have/Has + S +  V3 + ...?

9. 5 contoh future perfect tenses​


Jawaban:

Future perfect tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk kejadian yang telah selesai di masa depan. Jadi, kita menggunakan tense untuk membicarakan suatu kejadian di masa depan yang telah selesai. Rumus dari tense ini adalah

Subject + will have + past participle (v3)

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat future perfect tense yaitu

1. (+) I will have been here for six months on June 23rd.

(-)  I will not (won't) have been here for six months on June 23rd.

(?) Will I have been here for six months on June 23rd?

2. (+) By the time you read this I will have left.

(-) By the time you read this I won't have left.

(?) By the time you read this, will I will have left?

3. (+) You will have finished your report by this time next week.

(-) You won't have finished your report by this time next week.

(?) Will you have finished your report by this time next week?

4. (+) They will have arrived by 5:00.

(-) They won't have arrived by 5:00.

(?) Will they have arrived by 5:00?

5. (+) You will have eaten when I pick you up.

(-) You won't have eaten when I pick you up.

(?) Will you have eaten when I pick you up?

6. (+) You will have done your work by tomorrow

(-) You won't have done your work by tomorrow

(?) Will you have done your work by tomorrow?

7. (+) The parade will have ended by the time you get out of bed.

(-) The parade won't have ended by the time you get out of bed.

(?) Will the parade have ended by the time you get out of bed?

8. (+) He will have spent all the money before holiday ends.

(-) He won’t have spent all the money before holiday ends.

(?) Will he have spent all the money before holiday ends?

9. (+)  I will have reached the top achievement by the next five years.

(-) I won't have reached the top achievement by the next five years.

(?) Will I have reached the top achievement by the next five years?

10. (+) Mother will have retired from work by the time I finish college.

(-) Mother won't have retired from work by the time I finish college.

(?) Will mother have retired from work by the time I finish college?

11. (+) The teacher will have tolerated the same behavior if it happens again the next semester.

(-) The teacher won't have tolerated the same behavior if it happens again the next semester.

(?) Will the teacher have tolerated the same behavior if it happens again the next semester?

12. (+) She will have passed the test until she can answer all of the questions.

(-) She won’t have passed the test until she can answer all of the questions.

(?) Will she have passed the test until she can answer all of the questions?

13. (+) I will have booked the flight ticket by the time she asks me.

(-) I won’t have booked the flight ticket by the time she asks me.

(?) Will I have booked the flight ticket by the time she asks me?

14. (+) The children will have gone to bed when we arrive home.

(-) The children won't have gone to bed when we arrive home.

(?) Will the children have gone to bed when we arrive home?

15. (+) He will have sent the letter when we get home.

(-) He won't have sent the letter when we get home.

(?) Will he have sent the letter when we get home?

17. Creating simple past and present perfect tenses​


1.) Simple past:

(+) She bought a new doll two days ago.

(-) She didn't (did not) tell her childhood to me yesterday.

(?) Did She tell her childhood to me yesterday?

2.) Present perfect:

(+) They have done their duty just now

(-) They haven't (have + not) done their duty.

(?) Have they done their duty just now?

Penjelasan:

Simple past: Untuk menyatakan aktifitas yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Auxiliary yang digunakan yaitu: V² dan did.

Present perfect: Untuk menyatakan aktifitas yang sudah lengkap terjadi di masa yang baru saja terjadi.

Auxiliary yang digunakan yaitu: Have/ has dan V³.

23. cari definisi dan contohnya masing masing 3 dan buatlah kalimat Present tensesPresent continious tensesPresent perfect tensesPresent perfect continious tenses​


Jawaban:

# present tense = digunakan untuk menunjukkan fakta, kebiasaan, dan keadaan umum yang terjadi pada saat ini. contoh i work hard for this company atau she works hard for this company.

rumus (+) s + v(s/es) + o.

(-) s + do/does + not + verb + o

(?) do/does + s + verb + o

untuk subject i you they we pakai do dan verb tidak ditambah s/es, jika subject he she it pakai does dan verb ditambah s/es

# present continuous tense = digunakan untuk membicarakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung. Yang paling utama dari tense ini adalah adanya durasi waktu untuk kata kerjanya. contoh i'm eating now atau she is watching tv now.

rumus (+) s + is/am/are + verb ing + o

(-) s + is/am/are + not + verb ing + o

(?) is/am/are + s + verb ing + o

untuk i memakai am, untuk you they we memakai are, untuk he she it memakai is.

# present perfect tense = digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu aksi atau kejadian sudah selesai dan menekankan pada hasilnya. contoh i have finished my homework atau she has finished her homework.

rumus (+) s + have/has + v3 + o.

(-) s + have/has + not + v3 + o

(?) have/has + s + verb3 + o

untuk subject i you they we pakai have dan subject he she it pakai has.

# present perfect continuous = digunakan untuk menunjukkan aksi yang dimulai pada masa lampau dan terus berlanjut sampai saat ini. Biasanya aksi yang menggunakan tense ini mempunyai durasi waktu tentu dan mempunyai relevansi dengan kondisi sekarang. contoh i have been working here since 2015 atau she has been speaking for the last 3 hours.

rumus (+) s + have/has + been + verb ing + o

(-) s + have/has + not + been + verb ing + o

(?) have/has + s + been + verb ing + o

untuk subject i you they we pakai have dan subject he she it pakai has.

40. Contoh Kalimat past perfect tenses​


Answer :

Past perfect adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu kegiatan/perbuatan yang terjadi sebelum kegiatan/perbuatan lainnya pada masa lampau.

Rumus yang digunakan adalah :

(+) S + Had + V3 + O

(-) S + Had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O

Tanda waktu nya :

* After

* Before

* Dsb

Jika menggunakan after, maka :

..(simple past) after ...(past perfect)

Sedanglan jika menggunakan before, maka :

...(past perfect) before..(simple past)

Jadi, jika ada 2 kejadian dalam satu waktu, maka gunakan simple past di kalimat pertama & past perfect di kalimat kedua saat menggunakan "after", jika menggunakan "before" maka kebalikan dari "after".

Contoh :

I had studied english before my mother came home.I studied english after my mother had come home.
