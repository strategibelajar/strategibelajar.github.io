28. tolong kaka butu cepat....​


Jawaban:

a = 2,5 m/s² (C.)

Pembahasan:

Diketahui :

m1 = 30 kg

m2 = 10 kg

g = 10 m/s²

Ditanya : a (percepatan sistem) = ?

Jwb :

massa total (m) = m1 + m2 = 30 + 10 = 40 kg

ΣF = m2 × g = 10 × 10 = 100 N

a = ΣF / m = 100 / 40 = 2,5 m/s²
