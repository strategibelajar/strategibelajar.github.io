14. Contoh medical form , purchase form , order form, feservation form ?


Answer:

1. Medical form

Medical form is a form which is filled in by a patient to a doctor. This form contains the patient's personal health information, such as his/her illness, allergies, currently taken medicines, family health history, etc.

2. Purchase form

Purchase form is also known as purchase order (PO) form. Purchase form is a commercial document which is made by a buyer to a seller and used as a request to purchase products or services (normally in large quantities). This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products or service purchased.

3. Order form

Order form is a commercial document which is filled in by customers and used to order goods from a company. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products ordered.

4. Reservation form

Reservation form is a form which is filled in when booking or reserving something. it can be a hotel room, entertainment tickets, transportation, etc. This form contains the client's information, a number of things or places booked, and the date and time of when it's used. Some reservation forms require down payment as a reservation deal.

25. Definisi dari (bahasa inggris) - Purchase form - Order form - Withdrawal slip - Deposit slip - Reservation form - Medical form


Answer:

1. Purchase form

Purchase form is also known as purchase order (PO) form. Purchase form is a commercial document which is made by a buyer to a seller and used as a request to purchase products or services. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products or service purchased.

2. Order form

Order form is a commercial document which is filled in by customers and used to order goods from a company. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products ordered.

3. Withdrawal slip

Withdrawal slip is a piece of paper which is filled in by a client before withdrawing cash from an account. This slip is generally used in a bank. This slip contains the date of withdrawal, the client's name, the client's account number, the client's signature, and the amount of withdrawal.

4. Deposit slip

Deposit slip is a piece of paper which is filled in by a client before depositing cash into an account. This slip is generally used in a bank, too. This slip contains the date of depositing, the client's name, the client's account number, the amout of money deposited, and the client's signature.

5. Reservation form

Reservation form is a form which is filled in when booking or reserving something. it can be a hotel room, entertainment tickets, transportation, etc. This form contains the client's information, a number of things or places booked, and the date and time of when it's used. Some reservation forms requires down payment as a reservation deal.

6. Medical form

Medical form is a form which is filled in by a patient to a doctor. This form contains the patient's personal health information, such as his/her illness, allergies, currently taken medicines, family health history, etc.

39. apa saja manfaat dari form purchase order!


Answers

Purchase form is also know as purchase order (PO). This form is filled out by the buyer and then given to the buyer in order to process the order. This form indicates the descriptions, quantities, prices, discounts, payment terms, date of performance or shipment, other associated terms and conditions, and identifies a specific seller .

Below are some advantages of purchase form or PO.

1. It makes the order easy and quick to manage.

With the PO form, it's easier to track or follow up in case there is a problem and the buyer hasn't paid or hasn't received the items they bought. It's also very useful for the seller documentation.

2. Minimize error or mistake.

The form is easy to use. Buyer can fill in all of the details and do the recheck before submitting to the seller. It can minimize the error or mistake that might happen if there's no PO and the seller has to check every details of the customer.
