9. Berapa Ksp L(OH)2 yang mempunyai pH = 11​


Jawaban:

Ksp = 5 x 10^(-10)

Penjelasan:

L(OH)2 ---> L+ + 2OH- valensi = 2

pH = 11

pOH = 14-11 = 3

pOH = -log [OH-]

[OH-] = 10^(-3) M

M = [H+] / valensi

= 10^(-3) / 2

= 0,5 x 10^(-3) M

= 5 x 10^(-4) M

L(OH)2 ---------> L+ + 2OH-

5 x 10^(-4) s 2s

Ksp = s . (2s)^2

= s . 4s^2

= 4s^3

= 4 [5 x 10^(-4)]^3

= 4 x 125 x 10^(-12)

= 500 x 10^(-12)

= 5 x 10^(-10)

Contoh 1

Zn(OH)2 Zn2+ + 2OH-  

pOH = 14 – 9 = 5  

[OH-] = 10-5 M  

[Zn2+] =  

Ksp = [Zn2+] [OH-]2  

= 5 x 10-6 x 10-10  

= 5 x 10-16

[Mg2+] = 3 x 10-7 M

29. Jika Ksp Mg(OH)₂ = 3,2 x 10 ⁻¹¹ maka larutan akan jenuh pada pH?


Mg(OH)2  <--------> Mg^2+ + 2OH^-
   s                          s          2s

Ksp = [Mg^2+].[OH^-]^2
       = s.(2s)^2
       = 4s^3
s     = [tex] \sqrt[3]{3.2 x 10^-11}[/tex] / 4
       = [tex] \sqrt[3]{8 x 10^-12} [/tex]
       = 2 x 10^-4

OH^- = 2s
       = 2 (2 x 10^-4)
        = 4 x 10^-4
pOH = - log [OH^-]
       = -log [4x10^-4]
       = 4 - log 4

pH = 14 - pOH
     = 14 - (4- log 4)
      = 10 + log 4
