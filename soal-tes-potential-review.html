   H - TTB         C - TTB

---------------- = ---------------

TTA - TTB     TTA - TTB

  H - (-40)        30 - 0

---------------- = ------------

60 - (-40)       100 - 0

  H + 40       30

------------- = --------

 60 + 40      100

H + 40      3

---------- = -------

  100         10

10 (H + 40) = 100 x 3

10H + 400 = 300

10H = 300 - 400

10H = -100

H = -100/10

H = -10

800.000÷100.000= 8km

jadi 800.000 cm= 8 km (A)

jawab:

S= JP/JS

= 1/200.000

= 1:200.000 (D)

jawab:

JS= JP×S

= 4×100.000

= 400.000 cm → 4 km (A)

jawab:

JP= S×JS

= 1:300.000×600.000 cm
