15. Change the following sentences into modal passive sentences correctly!​


Jawaban:

1. If we had had the necessary ingredients for a cake, a cake would have been made by us.

2. The class should be attended by you more regularly.

3. English language can be read by her.

4. The most sophisticated car would be bought by me if I won million dollars.

5. The flight might have been taken by him yesterday morning.

6. A cheese cake will be baked by me for you if I have enough cheese.

7. The question would have been answered by him easily if he had listened to the teacher.

Penjelasan:

Dalam membentuk passive voice harus diperhatikan:

- verb selalu harus dalam bentuk verb-3

- dalam kalimat dengan modals verb maka rumus umumnya adalah:

a) subjek + modals + be + verb 3 (untuk bentuk tense simple, entah itu simple present, simple past atau simple future tense)

Contoh:

1 ) I will take the book (simple future, active)

   The book will be taken by me (passive)

2)  She can sing that song (simple present, active)

     Thet cong can be sung by her (passive)

3)  She could finally cook the cake yesterday. (simple past, active)

    The cake finally could be cooked by heryesterday. (passive)

b) subjek + modals + have + been + verb 3 (untuk bentuk perfect, entah itu present atau past, atau future perfect)

Contoh:

1) You will have passed the limit by this time tomorrow (future perfect tense, active)

  The limit will have been passed by you by this time tomorrow (passive)

2) He could have finished the work last night at 10 PM (past perfect, active)

  The work could have been finished by him last night at 10 PM (passive)

23. Changethe followingSentences intointerrogative.​


Jawaban:

1. How often does the man withdraw some money from his account?

2. When does one of the man want to see our manager?

3. Who always finish their homework on time?

4. How often does your friend teach English?

5. When do we start working every morning? / What time do we start working every morning?

Penjelasan:

Jika ada kalimat yang menyatakan "frequent" atau "seberapa sering" seperti "once a week", "twice a week", maka pertanyaan dapat diawali dengan "how often" yang berarti "seberapa sering". (no. 1 dan 4)Jika ada kalimat yang menyatakan waktu atau "kapan hal itu terjadi/dilakukan", maka pertanyaan dapat diawali dengan "when" yang artinya "kapan". (no. 2 dan 5)"Do" atau "does" dapat ditentukan dengan melihat subjek atau verb yang ada pada kalimat. Misal, jika subjeknya adalah he/she/it, maka pada interrogative sentence menggunakan "does" yang letaknya setelah 5W+1H sebagai awal kalimat. Sedangkan, jika dilihat dari verb (kata kerja) tidak ada "s" dibelakangnya, seperti "work" dan bukan "works", maka pada interrogative sentence menggunakan "do" yang letaknya setelah 5W+1H sebagai awal kalimat.He/she/it menggunakan "does".I/you/they/we menggunakan "do".Untuk yang no. 5, ada pembenaran grammar, seharusnya adalah "We always start working at 8 0'clock every morning."

40. change the following sentences into passive voice​


Jawaban:

Change the following sentences into passive voice.

1. Putri Permata usually telephones me on Saturdays.

Passive: I am usually telephoned by Putri Permata on Saturdays.

2. My sister cleans the room everyday.

Passive: The room is cleaned by my sister everyday.

3. My mother is washing the plates now.

Passive: The plates is being washed by my mother now.

4. The woman has helped the children.

Passive: The children have been helped by the woman.

5. The students have been doing their homework.

Passive: The students' homework has been being done (by them).

Penjelasan:

Pada soal nomor 1 dan 2: object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by subject (simple present tense)

Pada soal nomor 3: object + am/is/are + being + verb 3 + by subject (present continuous tense)

Pada soal nomor 4: object + has/have + been + verb 3 + by subject.

Pada soal nomor 5, yang di dalam kurung (by them), boleh ditulis tetapi lebih baik tidak ditulis karena pada object telah terdapat kata ganti subject-nya.
