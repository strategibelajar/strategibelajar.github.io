17. Jarak kota Banda Aceh dan Medan pada peta adalah 5 cm. Jika skala peta 1 : 8.500.000, berapa jarak sebenarnya kota Banda Aceh dan Medan?


❐ Skala dan Perbandingan

[tex] \rm \to 425~km [/tex]

_____________________________

Diketahui

[tex] \rm s = 1:8.500.000 [/tex]

[tex] \rm J_{p} = 5~cm [/tex]

[tex] \rm J_{s} = [/tex] ___?

Jarak sebenarnya,

[tex] \rm = 5~cm \times 8.500.000 [/tex]

[tex] \rm = 42.500.000~cm [/tex]

[tex] \rm = 425~km [/tex]

PEMBAHASAN

JP = Jarak pada Peta

JS = Jarak Sebenarnya

S = Skala

Rumus:

[tex]s = jp : js[/tex]

[tex]jp = s \times js[/tex]

[tex]js = jp \div s[/tex]

Diketahui:

JP = 5 cm

S = 1 : 8.500.000

Ditanya:

JS = ...

Jawaban:

[tex]js = jp \div s[/tex]

[tex]js = 5 \div \frac{1}{8.500.000} [/tex]

[tex]js = 5 \times 8.500.000[/tex]

[tex]js = 42.500.000 \: cm[/tex]

[tex]js = 42.500.000 \div 100.000 \: km[/tex]

[tex]js = 425 \: km[/tex]

Kesimpulan:

Jadi, jarak sebenarnya adalah 425 km

Penjelasan:

Lhoksemauwe is the second largest city in Aceh Special District, in the north of Sumatra , Indonesia . The city covers an area of 181.06 square kilometres, and had a population of 171,163 at the 2010 Census; the latest official estimate (as at 2014) is 180,200.Being between Bands Aceh and the large southern city of Medan , the town is a key regional centre important for the economy of Aceh.

History

The name of Lhokseumawe came from the words "Lhok" and "Seumawe". "Lhok" means deep, bay, ocean trenches and Lhokseumawe means the swirling water in the sea along the offshore of Banda Sakti and its surroundings. The city was formerly part of North Aceh Regency , The existence of this region can not be separated from the emergence of the kingdom Samudra Pasai around the 13th century, the area later became part of the sovereignty of Sultanate of Aceh since 1511.
