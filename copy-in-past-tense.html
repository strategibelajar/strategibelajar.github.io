Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Past Perfect Tense karena menjelaskan 2 kejadian di masa lampau, dimana kejadian pertama lebih dulu selesai sebelum kejadian ke dua di masa lampau.

Kejadian / peristiwa pertama yang terjadi terlebih dahulu menggunakan kata kerja bantu "had + Verb-3", baru diikuti peristiwa ke dua menggunakan Simple Past Tense.

Past tense is the tense which is used when we want to express something that happened or was done in the past time. The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past.

Pendahuluan:

Past tense is the tense which is used when we want to express something that happened or was done in the past time. The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past.

Formula :

A. Helping Verb [ With Example ]

(+) S + V² + O = I Was a Teacher

(?) V² + S + O = Was I a Teacher ?

(-) S + V² + Not + O = I Was not a Teacher

B. Full Verb [ With Example ]

(+) S + V² + O = She Drew an Apple

(?) Did + S + V¹ + O = Did She Draw an Apple?

(-) S + did + not + V¹ + O = She Did not Draw an Apple

Notes :

Keterangan waktu yang biasa dipakai adalah yesterday ( kemarin ) , last ( lalu ) , dan ago ( lalu ).

Untuk V² dibagi menjadi 2 jenis yaitu :

1) Regular Verbs ( Kata Kerja Beraturan )

Catatan : adding "d" or "ed"

Open = OpenedStudy = Studied

2. Irregular Verbs ( Kata Kerja Non Beraturan )

Catatan : not adding "d" or "ed"

Draw = DrewGo = Went

Untuk membaca V² yang berakhiran "ed" pada kata kerja Regular Verbs adalah dengan cara menekan ujung lidah tanpa harus dikeluarkan/membunyikannya secara jelas.

The Simple Past tense → actions that have already taken place in the past. V² dibagi menjadi 2 yaitu

40. Past Tense, Change the verbs in​


Jawaban:

1. Bob and I (be) at school yesterday.

(+) Bob and I were at school yesterday.

(-) Bob and I weren't at school yesterday.

(?) Were Bob and I at schoo yesterday?

2. I (have) breakfast this morning.

(+) I had breakfast this morning.

(-) I hadn't breakfast this morning.

(?) Had I breakfast this morning?

3. We (be) on time this morning.

(+) We were on time this morning.

(-) We weren't on time this morning.

(?) Were we on time this morning?

Penjelasan:

Simple past tense mengubah bentuk verb menjadi bentuk kedua. Namun apabila bentuk kalimatnya negatif (-) atau bentuk kalimatnya interogatif (?), maka bentuk verbnya tetap menjadi bentuk pertama.

Bentuk verb sendiri ada dua jenis, yakni:

Reguler verb: Dance - danced - dancedIrreguler verb: Speak - spoke - spoken

Sedangkan bentuk to be jika dalam keadaan lampau, maka menggunakan was dan were.

Examples:

Diana was at the mosque yesterday.

We were on time this morning.
