1. when was Julia found the thing? (kapan Julia menemukan benda itu?)

(apa yang wanita itu katakan kepada Julia?)

“oh, thank you very much,"

QUESTION:

5. what did the woman want to Julia?

(apa yang diinginkan wanita itu pada Julia?)

The woman wanted Julia to receive some of the money.

(Wanita itu ingin agar Julia menerima sejumlah uangnya)
