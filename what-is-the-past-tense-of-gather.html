1. What is the definition of Simple Past Tense? Write the examples! What is the difference between Simple Past Tense & Past Continuous Tense?


Definition of Simple Past Tense

The simple past is a verb tense that is used to talk about things that happened or existed before now.

Examples:

Verbal Simple Past Tense Sentence:

(+) The Doctor went to the hospital last monday. (-) The Doctor did not go to the hospital last monday. (?) Did the Doctor go to the hospital last monday?

Nominal Simple Past Tense Sentence:

(+) He was a doctor. (-) He was not a doctor. (?) Was he a doctor?

The difference between Simple Past Tense & Past Continuous Tense:Simple past tense, states that an action was done or occurred in the past. Past continuous tense, states that an action was happening at a certain time in the past. The action had started but was not finished at that time.

19. 1. What is the used of Simple Past tense? ​


The Simple Past tense is used to describe an action or event that occurred and was completed in the past. It is used to talk about past actions, habits, or states of being that are no longer true or relevant in the present. Here are some common uses of the Simple Past tense:

1. To talk about a specific action that happened at a definite time in the past:

Example: She studied for her exams last night.

2. To talk about a past habitual action or a repeated action:

Example: They always walked to school when they were young.

3. To talk about a series of completed actions in the past:

Example: He woke up, had breakfast, and went to work.

4. To narrate a story or describe a sequence of events in the past:

Example: Once upon a time, there was a brave knight who saved the princess.

5. To talk about past states or conditions:

Example: The weather was sunny yesterday.

It's important to note that the Simple Past tense is used for finished actions in the past and does not have a direct connection to the present.

40. what is the function of simple past tense


Simple Past Tense

JAWABAN:

The function of simple past tense is to indicate that an activity or situation began and ended at a particular time in the past.

(Fungsi dari simple past tense adalah untuk menunjukkan bahwa suatu kegiatan atau situasi dimulai dan berakhir pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu)

PEMBAHASAN:    

Dalam bahasa Inggris jika kita ingin menyampaikan suatu kegiatan atau situasi yang dimulai dan berakhir pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu maka kita harus menggunakan Simple Past Tense. Berikut penjelasan tentang Simple Past Tense.

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menunjukkan bahwa suatu kegiatan atau situasi dimulai dan berakhir pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2 (-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  (?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C (-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  (?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?
