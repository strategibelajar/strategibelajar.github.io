6. Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!​


Jawaban:

Kalimat "Find 2 lyrics of the song that use Present Perfect Tense and 5 lyrics that use Simple Past Tense!" berarti "Temukan 2 lirik lagu yang menggunakan Present Perfect Tense dan 5 lirik yang menggunakan Simple Past Tense!"

Kalian dapat mencari lirik lagu yang dimaksud menggunakan pola kalimat berikut sebagai acuan:

1.Present Perfect Tense

(+) S + have/has + Past participle atau S + has/have + been + O

(-) S + have/has not + Past participle atau S + has/have not + been + O

(?) Have/has + S + Past participle? atau has/have + S + been + O?

2.Simple Past Tense

(+) S + Verb 2 + O atau S + to be (was/were) + O

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 atau S + to be (was/were) not + O

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 atau Was/Were + S + . . . ?

Salah satu contoh lirik lagu tersebut adalah:

Present Perfect Tense

"Or have you completely erased me from your memory?", lirik lagu Don't You Remember - Adele.

Simple Past Tense

"You gave me wings and made me fly", lirik lagu Because you love me - Celine Dion.

16. Are lyrics and music equally important to a song or not


Jawaban:

important

Penjelasan:

because impoertamt

Jawaban:

Music can stand beautifully on its own without lyrics. There are a great number of songs that have no lyrics and yet proven to bring inexplicably calming vibes to the ears as in those of Mozart. Such songs are believed to be able to help people concentrate and sleep better. Having said that, lyrics are not necessarily less important than music. In fact, lyrics and music complement one another in a song. Moreover, lyrics in a song can be the reason why listeners listen to the same song over and over again. In this case lyrics are something they can relate to; they are more than just a bunch of words.

The elements of the song lyrics are divided into two, namely:

(1) The elements of form that can be referred to as physical structures, include among others:

- diction

- figuratively

- Pengimajian

- concrete words

- rhythm

- as well as typography.

(2) The elements of content or inner structure consist of:

- theme

- feelings

- tone

- and mandate.
