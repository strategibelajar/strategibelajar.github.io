21. The landlady made the tenants pay the rent on time. ##### The passive form of the sentence is ... * A.The landlady was made to pay the rent on time by the tenants B.The landlady made the rent pay on time C.The tenants were made the landlady paid the rent on time D.The tenants made the landlady pay the rent on time E.The tenants were made to pay the rent on time by the landlady


Jawaban:

The landlady made the tenants pay the rent on time.

The passive form of the sentence is ...

E. The tenants were made to pay the rent on time by the landlady

Penjelasan:

Perhatikan penjelasan berikut.

The landlady = subject

made = verb 2

the tenants = object (plural)

pay the rent on time = complement.

Rumus passive simple past tense adalah

Object + was/were + verb 3 + by subject + complement.

Sehingga:

The tenants (objek - plural) were (past auxiliary verb for plural) made (verb 3) by the landlady (subject) to pay the rent on time (complement).

Terdapat kata "to" pada "to pay" menunjukkan to infinitive pada satu kalimat yang memiliki dua kata kerja (were made dan to pay).

Flat fee is fixed fees that must be paid by the client whose amount will always be the same and is not affected by the number of services. It's the opposite of the Variabel Fee whose amount increases if the number of services also increases.

a : Variabel fee

b :Flat fee

x : number of letters

F : Total Fee

F = ax + b

26 = 35a + b

14,10 = 18a + b

------------------------ -

11,90 = 17a

a = 0,7 dollars (Variabel Fee)

26 = 35a + b

26 = 35(0,7) + b

26 = 24,5 + b

b = 1,5 dollars (Flat Fee)

Total Fee, when number of letters (x) =1

F = ax + b

F = 0,7(1) + 1,5

F = 2,2 dollars
