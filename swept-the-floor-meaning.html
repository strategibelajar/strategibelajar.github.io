Jawaban: B

Penjelasan:

20. “Tati is sweeping the floor” means

= Tati sedang menyapu lantai, artinya

A. Past Tense - Passive

(+) S + was/were + VERB 3

= The floor was swept

B. Present Continuous - Passive

(+) S + is/am/are + being + verb 3

= The floor is being swept

C. Present Perfect

(+) S + has/have + verb 3

= the floor has swept

D. Past Continuous - Passive

(+) S + was/were + being + Verb 3

= the floor was being swept

Alasannya memilih B.

Karena kalimat Soal bentuk Present Continuous - Active

(+) S + is/am/are + Verb+ing + O

= Tati + is + sweeping + the floor

(Tati sedang menyapu lantai)

Maka Passive:

(+) S + is/am/are + being + Verb3

= the floor + is + being + swept

(Lantai sedang disapu)

Jadi:

Kedua kalimat tersebut adalah SEARTI atau memiliki makna yg SAMA yaitu Lantai sedang disapu.

Category : Part of Speech

Pelengkap itu complement ya?
Complement ada 3 macam :
1. subject complement = letaknya sesudah linking verb (×)
2. object complement = letaknya sesudah direct object (×)
3. verb complement = objek (×)

jadi "by me" dan "yesterday" bukan complement ataupun object.
mereka adalah adverb (keterangan).
by me = keterangan pelaku
