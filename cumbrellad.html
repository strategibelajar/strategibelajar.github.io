24. a raincoat cost 4 times as much as an umbrella. Mrs Gomez paid a total of56 for 3 identical umbrellas and a raincoat.What was the difference in price between the raincoat andthe umbrella?​


Jawaban:

24

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

let price of raincoat = r

and price of umbrella = u

r = 4u ...(i)

3u+r = 56 ...(ii) -> substitute (i)

3u+4u = 56

7u = 56

u = 8

r = 4u

r= 4.8

r = 32

so price of raincoat is 32

and price of umbrella is 8

the difference in price is = 32-8 = 24
