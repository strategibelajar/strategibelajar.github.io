12. Thank you for ... helpful advice. I feel better now. Glad you found it so helpful. A. Such a B. Such an C. Such D. Such the E. Such so


DETERMINER

Thank you for such helpful advice. I feel better now.  

-----------------

EXPLANATION

Advice adalah kata benda yang tidak dapat dihitung atau uncountable noun. Oleh karena itu advice tidak menggunakan kata sandang 'a' ataupun 'an'. Jadi, opsi A dan B salah.

Kata sandang 'the' tidak dapat digunakan bersama determiner 'such'. Jadi opsi D salah.

Determiner 'such' dan 'so' mempunyai arti yang sama, oleh karena itu tidak bisa digunakan bersamaan. Jadi, opsi E salah.

Jawaban yang tepat adalah opsi C. such.

*'advice' dapat berupa countable noun jika yang dimaksud adalah 'advice' dalam bahasa pemograman komputer.

36. complete the following sentences by using so... that and such.. that. 1. this box is so heavy... 2. that car is so expensive... 3. the coffee was so hot.... 4. it is too dark in here....... 5. nunik was walking so slowly.... . 6. it was such a heavy congestion.... 7 . it was such an intelligent article..... 8 we are studying in such a highly reputable school...... 9.the hotel served such bad food..... 10. it was such a boring subject...... .


Completing sentence by using so…that and such…that adalah sebagai berikut.


1. This box is so heavy that I am not able to carry it.

2. That car is so expensive that most of people cannot afford it.

3. The coffee was so hot that I have to be careful drinking it.

4. It is too dark in here that nobody can see anything.

5. Nunik was walking so slowly that even a turtle walks faster.

6. It was such a heavy congestion that everyone stuck inside the car for at least three hours.

7. It was such an intelligent article that deserved to be published on the international journal.

8. We are studying in such a highly reputable school that almost every students graduate from there becomes a successful person.

9. The hotel served such bad food that even I wouldn’t give it to my dog.

10. It was such a boring subject that I always fall asleep during the lesson.


Pembahasan

Kita menggunakan ungkapan so…that dan such…that untuk menunjukan sebuah fakta yang biasanya diikuti oleh hasil atau sebuah konsekuensi.


Berikut adalah beberapa formula yang bisa kalian gunakan.


1. So + adjective/adverb + that + result

Contoh: Our teacher speaks so loud that everyone can hear him.


2. Such + a + (adjective) + singular noun + that + result

Contoh: They had such a bad nightmare that they couldn’t sleep.


3. Such + plural/uncountable noun + that + result

Contoh: It was such a hot weather that I sweat after one minute.
