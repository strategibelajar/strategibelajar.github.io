In other words, they believe that the video games can actually sharpen a human’s brain.Conversely, some people believe that video games make children lazy. They argue that playing video games is addictive. As a consequence, most of the children prefer playing video games to studying. In fact, there are also some children who skip their extracurricular lessons, just because they want to play video games. In addition, most of the video games often expose too much violence. Two years ago in the USA, for example, a student shot his teacher and some of his friends. 28. children should be allowed to play an hour of video games daily. minimum 9 lines


Jawaban:

Many parents think that children should not be allowed to play video games. But that is because many parents only see the negative side of playing video games. In fact there are many benefits children could get from playing video games. Recent research has proved that playing video games could develop cognitive skills on children. There are some of the cognitive benefits of playing video games: improves coordination, improves problem-solving skills, enhances memory, improves attention and concentration, etc. Though video games might be beneficial, there is need to play them in moderation. It is also important to pick the right video games as not all of them provide the same cognitive benefits. Age should also be a factor, small children should not be exposed to violent games. And time control is a must, one hour per day should be enough to avoid video games addiction.

Banyak orang tua yang beranggapan bahwa anak sebaiknya tidak diperbolehkan untuk bermain video games. Tapi itu karena kebanyakan orang tua hanya melihat sisi negatif dari bermain video games. Faktanya banyak manfaat yang bisa didapatkan oleh anak dari bermain video games. Penelitian terkini membuktikan bahwa bermain video games dapat mengembangkan kemampuan kognitif pada anak. Terdapat beberapa manfaat kognitif yang didapat dari bermain video games seperti: meningkatkan daya koordinasi, meningkatkan kemampuan memecahkan masalah, meningkatkan daya ingat. meningkatkan daya fokus dan konsentrasi, serta lainnya. Meskipun memiliki manfaat seperti itu, terdapat batasan yang diperlukan dalam memainkannya. Sangatlah penting untuk memilih video games yang tepat, karena tidak semuanya memberikan manfaat kognitif yang sama. Usia juga dapat mejadi faktor penentu, anak kecil tidak seharusnya dikenalkan dengan game kekerasan. Dan juga diperlukan kontrol waktu, 1 jam perhari seharusnya sudah cukup untuk menghindari kecanduan video games.
