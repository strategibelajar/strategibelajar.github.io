5. Look at the sentences you made for task 2 of this section. now, make extended statements using present perfect tense. look at the example below.


Present Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung sebelum sekarang (lampau).

Perbedaan present perfect tense dengan simple past tense adalah present past tense tidak membutuhkan keterangan waktu lampau yang spesifik seperti yesterday, last week, last night, this morning, dan lain-lain.

Keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan dalam present perfect tense adalah:

since (diikuti dengan tanggal, bulan atau tahun yang pasti). contoh since 1991, since March, Since monday, dan lain lainFor (diikuti dengan durasi waktu). Contoh: for two weeks, for three years, for three hours, dan lain lain.

Pattern present perfect tense adalah:

(+) S+ has/have + V3

(-) S + has/have not + V3

(?) Has/have + S + V3?


Has untuk subject He, She, It

Have untuk subject I, You, They, We


Pembahasan

Buatlah kalimat lengkap[ menggunakan present perfect tense

I have bought a new car since I work at the bank. My sister has started a new job since August. She has already submitted her paper since last Monday. We have been to Singapore since last Tuesday. Peter has gone to the bookstore for two hours. There has been an accident near here. My father has broken his leg since he was 40. My students have improved their English since I taught them. They have had a newborn baby for two weeks. My friends and I have worked hard for last three years.

Present Perfect Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan ataupun perbuatan yang terjadi pada waktu yang tidak tertentu di masa lampau

_________________________

Rumus Verbal

(+) S + Have/Has + Verb 3

Example

She has taught this class for three years

(-) S + Have/Has + Not + Verb 3

Example

She has not taught this class for three years

(?) Have/Has + S + Verb 3

Example

Has she taught this class for three years?

Notes

• Have

Digunakan untuk subjek I/We/They/You

• Has

Digunakan untuk subjek She/He/It
