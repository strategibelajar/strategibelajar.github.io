35. Ubalah dalam pangkat positif ​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

A :

[tex] {2}^{ - 4} \\ \\ = \frac{1}{ {2}^{4} } [/tex]

B :

[tex] \frac{2}{ {p}^{ - 3} } \\ \\ = 2 {p}^{3} [/tex]

Ingat 2 rumus di bawah ini :

[tex]1. \: {a}^{ - b} = \frac{1}{ {a}^{b} } \\ \\ 2. \: \frac{1}{ { a}^{ - b} } = {a}^{b} [/tex]

Jawaban:

[tex]a) \: \: ( { \frac{1}{2} })^{4} [/tex]

[tex]b) \: \: 2 {p}^{ - 3} [/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]a) \: \: {2}^{ - 4} [/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: = ( \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} )[/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: = \frac{1}{ {2}^{4} } [/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: jadi \: jawabannya \: ({ \frac{1}{2} })^{4} [/tex]

[tex]b) \: \: \frac{2}{ {p}^{ - 3} } [/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: = 2 \div {p}^{ - 3} [/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: = 2 {p}^{ - 3} [/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: jadi \: jawabannya \: adalah \: 2 {p}^{ - 3} [/tex]

Keterangan:

Pada nomor 1, jika pangkatnya negatif maka bilangan diubah menjadi pecahan, sedangkan pada nomor 2 karena bilangannya beda variabel, maka digabungkan saja

40. ubalah ke passive voice


ACTIVE-PASSIVE VOICE

Kalimat aktif adalah kalimat yang subyeknya melakukan aktivitas/pekerjaan tertentu, sedangkan pada kalimat pasif subyeknya tidak melakukan aktivitas melainkan menerima aksi.

Contoh dalam kalimat sebagai berikut :

She brings a lot of books to the class ⇒ Aktif

A lot of books are brought by her to the class ⇒ Pasif

Mari kita lihat formula pembentukan kalimat pasif dari kalimat aktif :

Simple Present Tense

Aktif : Subject +Verb 1+ Direct object

Pasif : Direct object + is/am/are/+past participle+ by...

Present Continuous Tense

Aktif : S+ is/am/are+ present continuous+direct object

Pasif : S(Direct object) + is/am/are+being+past participle+by..

Present Perfect Tense

Aktif : S + have/has + present participle + direct object

Pasif : S (Direct Object) + have/has + been + past participle + by...

Simple Past Tense

Aktif : S + Verb-2 + Direct Object

Pasif : S (Direct Object) + was/were + past participle + by..

Past Continuous Tense

Aktif : S + was/were + present participle + direct object

Pasif : S (Direct Object) + was/were + being + past participle+by...

Simple Future Tense

Aktif : S + will+ Verb-1+direct object

        S + am/is/are going to + bare infinitive + direct object

Pasif : S (Direct Object) + am/is/are going to + be + past participle + by.....

Pembahasan

1. Karin has shed the tears since you left her for Bianca.

 The tears have been shed by Karin since you left her for Bianca.

2. Mark wore a Dracula's outfit at the Halloween party.

  A Dracula's outfit was worn by Mark at the Halloween party.

3. Mother was wringing the wet towel when I found her at the back yard.

  The wet towel was being wrung by mother when I found her at the backyard.

4. Indonesia has been building a new stadium for the next Sea Games.

  A new stadium had been built for the next Sea Games by Indonesia.

5. Mika will be sowing the chili seeds when Bram calls her.

  The chili seeds are going to be sown by Mika when Bram calls her.

6. I had turn off the lights before I went to bed.

   The lights had been turned off by me before I went to bed.

7. Billy always spills the coffee he drinks.

  The coffee he drinks is always spilt by him.

8. You raise me up when I'm down.

  I am raised up by you when I'm down.

9. He could tell the incident in detail.

   The incident in detail could be told by him.

10. A stranger took Riska home last night.

   Riska is taken home by a stranger last night.
