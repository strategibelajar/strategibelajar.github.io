2. what do you have to do first when you want to use a fax machine​


Jawaban:

1. Make sure the fax machine is plugged into a power source and also plugged into a working phone jack.

2. Turn the fax machine on.

Obtain the fax number of the destination fax machine.

3. Gather the documents you want to send and put them in the order you want them to be received.

4. Fill out a separate piece of paper called a coversheet with the recipient's name, fax number/phone number, your name, your phone number, a short message and number of pages (including coversheet).

5. Lay the documents face-up in the fax machine feeder tray with the coversheet on top

6. Dial the recipient's fax number (dialing instructions for international calls)

7. Press the "fax" or "send" button, depending on the particular fax machine model

Jawaban:

1. First of all, prepare the document you want

to fax and make sure the destination number is

correct. If so, then the document is inserted into

the fax machine feeder. Place the sheet

precisely and straight, not at an angle.

2. After the paper is in the right position, press

the numeric buttons on the fax machine

(such as telephone numbers) according to the

destination number. Don't get it wrong

because it will be difficult to tell if you send

it wrong. After pressing the destination number

on the fax machine, next you only need to

press the send button. The size of this button

is usually slightly larger than other buttons.

3. Then the sheet of paper will be pulled in by

the fax machine feeder slowly, indicating

the fax process is in progress. After the fax

is complete, you will receive a confirmation

whether your document was sent successfully

or not.


3. what do you have to do frirst when you want to use a fax machine


To send a fax with your fax machine:

1. Place the document you want to send in the document feeder. There is usually a small icon that will show which was is “face up” for sending your document.
2. Enter the fax number you want to send to, including and extensions to dial externally, and any international dialing codes
3. Press Send or Go (depending on your fax machine model)
4. Wait for the fax to finish scanning and sending your document
5. Take your confirmation page (if you have the feature enabled)
6. Take your original document with you
