Penjelasan:

Percakapan pada soal menggunakan kalimat present continuous tense & simple future tense.

Present continuous tenses adalah tenses yang berfungsi untuk menerangkan suatu peristiwa pada masa sekarang yang sedang berlangsung.

Simple future tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan kejadian yang belum terjadi atau akan terjadi di masa depan dan berakhir di masa depan.

Rumusnya

Present continuous tense

(+) S + to be + V-ing

contoh: I'm trying to get this peace of toast out of the toaster.

(-) S + to be + not + V-ing

contoh: I'm not trying to get this peace of toast out of the toaster.

(?) To be + S + V-ing?

contoh: what are you doing?

Simple future tense

(+) S + will/shall + V1 + complement

contoh: you will electrocute yourself.

(-) S + will + not + V1 + complement

contoh: you will not electrocute yourself.

(?) Will/shall + S + V1 + complement

contoh: will you electrocute yourself?

20. Back To School - Tolong bantu jawab ya. Honestly, there's ____ a skill shortage ____ companies are paying really good money now.


answer

2 but

selesaiiAnswer :
Honestly, there's SUCH a skill shortage THAT companies are paying really good money now.

Adverbs of degree So and Such
So is placed before an adjective/adjective phrase.
e.g.
He is so tall.
She was so highly praised.

Such is placed before a noun phrase (adjective/adverb + noun)
e.g.
He is such a tall man.
She was such an amazing woman.
They are such smart students.

'a skill shortage' is a noun; to  modify it, use 'such'.

Berapa banyak jumlah Try Out seluruhnya yang diikuti Tony ?


anggap aja

n = bnyak tryout

x = jmlah nilai tryout sebelumnya

berarti :

Xn + 80 / (n+1) = 85..

Xn + 80 = 85n + 85

Xn = 85n + 5...(1)

Xn + 95 /(n+1) = 88

Xn + 95 = 88n + 88

Xn = 88n - 7...(2)

eliminasi deh.

Xn = 85n + 5

Xn = 88n - 7

---------------------- -

0 = -3n + 12

-3n = -12

n = 12/3

n = 4

35. seorang siswa terpandai SSC mendapat nilai try out (TO) berturut-turut 78,86,88,91. berapa nilai TO selanjutnya (kelima) yang harus diperoleh agar rata-rata try out 85?


Seorang siswa terpandai SSC mendapat nilai try out (TO) berturut-turut 78,86,88,91. Berapa nilai TO selanjutnya (kelima) yang harus diperoleh agar rata-rata try out 85. Maka nilai selanjutnya adalah 82.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah

Diketahui :

Siswa terpandai SSC mendapat nilai try out (TO) berturut-turut 78,86,88,91

Ditanya : Nilai TO selanjutnya (kelima) yang harus diperoleh agar rata-rata try out 85.

Jawab :

Rata2 = 85

Total ujian = 5

Nilai keseluruhan = 425

Nilai yg sudah didapat = 78+86+88+91 = 343

Nilai ujian ke-5 = 425-343 = 82

Jadi, nilai TO selanjutnya (kelima) yang harus diperoleh agar rata-rata try out 85 adalah 82
