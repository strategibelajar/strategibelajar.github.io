16. It - run - can - fast- and - has - ears - long​


Jawaban:

Arranged:

It has long ears and (it) can run fast.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan sudah disusun menjadi sebuah kalimat yang memiliki makna dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kata ganti subjek (it) ditambahkan karena kalimat memiliki kata ganti subjek yang sama dan kata benda yang digantikan dengan kata ganti subjek "it" tersebut adalah kata "Rabbit" yang artinya kelinci karena kelinci memiliki teinga-telinga yang panjang (long ears) dan bisa berlari cepat (it can run fast).

Kalimat menggunakan dua bentuk, yaitu Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan fakta dan Modal Verbs (Can) karena menjelaskan kemampuan.

Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Modal Verbs (Can):

(+) Subject + Can + Verb-1 + Object

Semoga membantu ya.

1.Australia
2. In addition, marsupial has front pouch, which contains multiple nipples for the protection and sustenance of their young, marsupials have other common structural features
3.baby kangaroos are highly underdeveloped and embryo like at birth. After a gestation of up to 34 days, the jellybean-sized youngster makes the journey from birth canal to pouch by clambering up through its mother’s fur. Once safely in the pouch, the joey suckles solidly for just over two months.

At around six months, once the youngster is sufficiently well developed, it will leave the pouch for short periods, returning when it needs to feed. Red kangaroos leave the pouch for good at around eight months and continue to suckle for another three to four months; grey kangaroos leave at about 11 months, continuing to suckle until they are as old as 18 months.

Interestingly, female kangaroos are able to suckle two youngsters simultaneously. one in the pouch and one outside, offering two different types of milk, as well as having an egg ready for implantation
