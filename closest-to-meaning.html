Penjelasan:

In English, some words can be regarded as formal and informal. They are used based on the context. The word 'belonging', which is also the synonym of 'thing', is more suitable for the formal context, such as announcements or notice and warning on public places. Here are the examples:

Please leave your belongings outside the classroom before entering the exam chamber.(at school)Put all your belongings in the available container at the airport security check. (at the airport)

In Bahasa Indonesia, 'belonging' means 'barang', which is the same as 'thing', 'object' or 'stuff'.
