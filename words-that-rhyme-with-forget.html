6. A rhyme is when....... A. a word from two words or phrases ends in the same sounds B. two words from phrases begins with the same letter C. two words from two line ends with different sounds D. a word from two lines are the same


Answer:

To answer a question which doesn’t have any further explanation or given clue, we don’t have any other choice except collecting some information regarding to the keyword. We can get the information about the keyword by looking for it on the internet. The keyword is rhyme.

Rhyme is a repetition of similar sounds at the end of each line in a poem. The example of rhyme:

Humpty Dumpty sat on a wall,
Humpty Dumpty had a great fall.

From the explanation above we can conclude that both lines are ended with the similar sound.

So the best answer is: A. a word from two words or phrases ends in the same sounds

28. An international magazine has organised a short story competition and you have decided to enter. The competition rules say that the story must begin or end with the following words: Susan will never forget what happened that day.​


Jawaban:

Susan will never forget what happened that day. It was a bright summer morning, the sun casting a golden glow over the picturesque town where she lived. As she walked down the familiar streets, a sense of anticipation filled the air. The annual summer fair was in full swing, attracting locals and visitors alike.

Susan's heart raced with excitement as she joined the bustling crowd. The fairgrounds were adorned with colorful banners and vibrant stalls selling all sorts of delights. The aroma of cotton candy and popcorn wafted through the air, tempting her senses.

Amidst the cheerful chaos, Susan's eyes fell upon a peculiar old fortune teller booth. Its weathered sign proclaimed, "Fortunes Revealed: Past, Present, and Future." Intrigued, Susan hesitated for a moment before stepping inside.

The air inside the booth felt heavy with mystery. A cloaked figure emerged from the shadows, beckoning Susan to sit at the small table. The fortune teller's piercing eyes seemed to hold a profound wisdom, drawing her in.

Gently, the fortune teller took Susan's hand and began tracing the lines on her palm. Their voice resonated with an otherworldly calmness as they foretold Susan's past, recounting moments she had long forgotten. The revelations stirred deep emotions within her, reminding her of the journey she had traversed.

But it was when the fortune teller gazed into Susan's future that her heart skipped a beat. A vision unfolded before her eyes, revealing a path of unexpected twists and turns. Susan realized that the choices she made that day would shape her destiny in ways she could not yet comprehend.

Leaving the booth, Susan carried the weight of newfound knowledge and a sense of purpose. She wandered through the fair, reflecting on the profound encounter. The vibrant colors and joyful laughter seemed to echo the possibilities that lay ahead.

As the sun began to set, casting an orange glow over the fairgrounds, Susan knew that her life would never be the same. The words of the fortune teller lingered in her mind, fueling her determination to embrace the unknown with courage and resilience.

Susan will never forget what happened that day—the day she discovered that the tapestry of her life was woven with threads of destiny, waiting to be unraveled with every step she took into the future.
