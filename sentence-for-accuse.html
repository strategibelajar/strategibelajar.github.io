Jawaban:

1. Accuse

Simple Present : She accuses everyone of stealing her pen.

Present Progressive : She is accusing everyone of stealing her pen now.

Simple Past : She accused everyone of stealing her pen yesterday.

Past Progressive : She was accusing everyone of stealing her pen the whole day.

Present Perfect : She has just accused everyone of stealing her pen.

Present Perfect Progressive : She has been accusing everyone of stealing her pen for 5 hours.

Past Perfect : She had accused everyone of stealing her pen before school was over.

Past Perfect Progressive : She had been accusing everyone of stealing her pen when school was over.

Future - Will : She will accuse everyone of stealing her pen tomorrow.

Future - Going To : She is going to accuse everyone of stealing her pen when school is over.

Future Progressive : She will be accusing everyone of stealing her pen after school.

Future Perfect : She will have accused everyone of stealing her pen by tomorrow.

Conditional Simple : She would accuse everyone of stealing her pen.

Conditional Progressive : She would be accusing everyone of stealing her pen.

Conditional Perfect : She would have accused everyone of stealing her pen.

Conditional Perfect Progressive : She would have been accusing everyone of stealing her pen.

2. Acquit

Simple Present : He acquits on all counts.

Present Progressive : He is acquiting on all counts.

Simple Past : He acquited on all counts.

Past Progressive : He was acquiting on all counts.

Present Perfect : He has just acquitted on all counts.

Present Perfect Progressive : He has been acquitting on all counts.

Past Perfect : He had acquitted on all counts.

Past Perfect Progressive : He had been acquitting on all counts.

Future - Will : He will acquit on all counts.

Future - Going To - He is going to acquit on all counts.

Future Progressive : He will be acquitting on all counts.

Future Perfect : He will have acquitted on all counts.

Conditional Simple : He would acquit on all counts.

Conditional Progressive : He would be acquitting on all counts.

Conditional Perfect : He would have acquitted on all counts.

Conditional Perfect Progressive : He would have been acquitting on all counts.

20. Change the sentences bellow into passif. 1. We clean our class every day. 2. She invite me to the party. 3. They cancelled all flights because of fog. 4. We didn't accuse you of stealing money. 5. He solved Math problem very quickly...tolong dibantu ya teman-teman​


Bentuk pasif dari kalimat di atas adalah :

Jawaban :
1). Active : we CLEAN our class every day.
Passive : Our class IS CLEANED by us every day.

2). Active : She INVITES me to the party.
Passive : I AM INVITED to the party by her.

3). Active : They CANCELLED all flights because of fog.
Passive : All flights WERE cancelled by them because of fog.

4). Active : We DIDN'T ACCUSE you of stealing money.
Passive : You WEREN'T ACCUSED of stealing money by us.

5). Active : he SOLVED math problem very quickly.
Passive : Math problem WAS SOLVED by him very quickly.

Tenses yang digunakan pada kalimat di atas adalah :

1). Simple Present
Active : S + V1 (s/es) + O
Passive : S + To be (am/are/is) + V3 + by...

2). Simple present
3). Simple Past positive
Active : S + V2 + O
Passive : S + To be (was/were) + V3 + by...

4). Simple past negative
Active : S + DID + NOT + V1 + O
Passive : S + To be (was/were) + NOT + V3 + by...

5). Simple past positive

Catatan !!!
=> Subject di kalimat aktif berubah jadi object di kalimat pasif, begitu pula sebaliknya.
