27. like ... better than ... = prefer ... to ...like ... better than ... and prefer ... to ... are used to show preference.example:i like chess better than rugby [l prefer chess to rugby].she likes swimming better than hiking (she prefers swimming to hiking).tonumber onetask 3 change like ... better than ... into preferhas been done for you.1. anton likes playing tennis better than playing golf.[anton prefers playing tennis to playing golf.]2. you like football better than baseball.3. belinda likes surfing better than sailing.4. mr. tom likes running better than swimming.5. anne and jacky like softball better than squash.6. she likes golf better than horse-riding.7. my brother likes squash better than badminton.tolongg​


Change like ... better than ... into prefer~

1. anton likes playing tennis better than playing golf.

Answer (Jawaban)

=> [anton prefers playing tennis to playing golf.]

2. you like football better than baseball.

Answer

=> [you prefer football to baseball.]

3. belinda likes surfing better than sailing.

Answer

=> [belinda prefers surfing to sailing.]

4. mr. tom likes running better than swimming.

Answer

=> [mr. tom prefers running to swimming.]

5. anne and jacky like softball better than squash.

Answer

=> [anne and jacky prefer softball to squash.]

6. she likes golf better than horse-riding.

Answer

=> [she prefers golf to horse-riding.]

7. my brother likes squash better than badminton.

Answer

=> [my brother prefers squash to badminton.]

~

Explanation (Penjelasan):

Better than ... = Prefer to ...

are used to show preference.

example(contoh):

-> I like chess better than rugby [l prefer chess to rugby].

-> she likes swimming better than hiking [she prefers swimming to hiking].

"Prefer" = digunakan pada subject he (dia laki-laki), she (dia perempuan), dan it (kata benda).

"Prefers" = digunakan pada subject I, you, we, they, dan noun berbentuk jamak.


28. it is said that figs are better than apples, bgaimana diubah menjadi kalimat aktiv?


(+)it is said that figs are better than apple's
(-)it is said that figs are not better than apple's
(?)is it said that figs are better than apple's?
maaf klo salah Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: passive voice
Pembahasan:

The active form of the sentence ' it is said that figs are better than apple' is
' They say/ people say that figs are better than apple.'

In that kind of reported sentence, only the beginning of the sentence which can be transformed into passive or active ('they say/ believe/ think' can be changed into ' it is said/ believed / thought' for passive ) . The remaining part of the sentence stays the same.
