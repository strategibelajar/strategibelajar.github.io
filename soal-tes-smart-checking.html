Gradien Garis = (2 - (-6) / (-1 - 3)

Gradien Garis = (2 + 6) / -4

Gradien Garis = 8 / -4

b. M(-6,4) dan N(2,-2)

x1 = -6

x2 = 2

y1 = 4

y2 = -2

Gradien Garis = (y² - y¹) / (x² - x¹)

Gradien Garis = (-2 - 4) / (2 - (-6)

Gradien Garis = -6 / 2 + 6

Gradien Garis = -6 / 8
