13. lim 2x²-x-3/x²-3 ? lim x³-2x²/x²+x-6?lim x⁴-4x²/x²+x-6?dengan caranya​


Berikut ini adalah hasil pengerjaan limit aljabar.

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to -1} \frac{2x^2-x-3}{x^2-2x-3} = \frac{5}{4}}[/tex][tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3-2x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \frac{4}{5} }[/tex][tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^4-4x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \frac{16}{5} }[/tex]Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Soal No.1

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to -1} \frac{2x^2-x-3}{x^2-2x-3} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{(2x-3)(x+1)}{(x-3)(x+1)} }[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to -1} \frac{2x^2-x-3}{x^2-2x-3} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{2x-3 }{x-3}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to -1} \frac{2x^2-x-3}{x^2-2x-3} = \frac{2(-1)-3 }{(-1)-3}}[/tex]

∴ [tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to -1} \frac{2x^2-x-3}{x^2-2x-3} = \frac{5}{4}}[/tex]

Soal No.2

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3-2x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2(x-2)}{(x+3)(x-2)} }[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3-2x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2}{x+3} }[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3-2x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \frac{2^2}{2+3} }[/tex]

∴ [tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3-2x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \frac{4}{5} }[/tex]

Soal No.3

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^4-4x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2(x^2-4)}{(x+3)(x-2)} }[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^4-4x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2(x+2)(x-2)}{(x+3)(x-2)} }[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^4-4x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2(x+2)}{x+3} }[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^4-4x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \frac{2^2(2+2)}{2+3} }[/tex]

∴ [tex]\boxed{ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^4-4x^2}{x^2+x-6} = \frac{16}{5} }[/tex]
