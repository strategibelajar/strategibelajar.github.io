Jika Tony mendapatkan nilai 80 pada try out berikutnya, rata-rata try out Tony adalah 85. Sedangkan jika Tony mendapatkan nilai 95 pada try out berikutnya, rata-ratanya menjadi 88. Berapa banyak jumlah Try Out seluruhnya yang diikuti Tony ?


anggap aja

n = bnyak tryout

x = jmlah nilai tryout sebelumnya

berarti :

Xn + 80 / (n+1) = 85..

Xn + 80 = 85n + 85

Xn = 85n + 5...(1)

Xn + 95 /(n+1) = 88

Xn + 95 = 88n + 88

Xn = 88n - 7...(2)

eliminasi deh.

Xn = 85n + 5

Xn = 88n - 7

---------------------- -

0 = -3n + 12

-3n = -12

n = 12/3

n = 4

berarti tony 4 x ngikut tryout..

24. apa pengertian giving opinion dalam bahasa iInggris? Dan Berikan contohnya


Opinion is a phrase or sentence consist of argument and reason about certain issue. Giving opinion is a sentence or expression that gives a reason to someone or other people about your personal/general point of view.

Pembahasan

Ketika akan memberikan opini atau pendapat mengenai suatu topik/isu ada beberapa kalimat ungkapan yang bisa kalian gunakan disini sebagai awalan. Contoh:

I think….I believe….According to me….In my view….In my opinion….From my perspective….From my point of view….Personally, I think….Some people say that….

Berikut adalah contoh cara memberikan opini berdasarkan situasi tertentu:

1. What do you think about the Jakarta’s MRT that will be launched soon on 2019?

Answer: I think it’s a great thing that finally the capital has its own effective and fast transportation system. Jakarta’s people will no longer have to struggle with traffic jams and crowded public transportation anymore.

2. How do you feel about the corruption in Indonesia?

Answer: From my perspective, it’s very sad that corruption has been one of the major problem in Indonesia which eventually causing poverty. I believe that Indonesia could be a better country one day, but together we as a nation should eradicate corruption as soon as possible.
