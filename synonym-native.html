24. Apakah perbedaan dari upscaled resolution dan native resolution?


bedanya simple sebenarnya. kalau native, antara resolusi yang dikirim dari vga ke monitor sama. kalau upscale, resolusi yang dikirim vga lebih kecil dibanding resolusi monitor.

sebagai contoh.
saya punya pc dengan vga support 1366x768.
dihubungkan ke display lcd monitor dengan res 1366x768. ini adalah kondisi optimal alias native res.
gambar yang terlihat dimonitor lebih smooth.

jika vga hanya support 800x600 sementara lcd sudah 1366x768 maka gambar akan terlihat pecah atau kotak2. karena pixelnya nggak sama. ini yang namanya upscale.

The synonym of the word 'native' as a noun are
- local
- national
- aborigine
Example:
- Joao, our new colleague, is also a native Portuguese speaker despite having a French passport. 

Meanwhile the synonym of ' native ' as adjective are
- original
- aboriginal
- indigineous
- natural
- inborn
- essential
Example:
- Tagalog is Filipino's native language or mother tongue.

29. apa synonym dari had


Have, own, posses

Maaf kalau kurang nyambung

Question and answer :

What is the synonym of had?

Synonyms of had are possessed, owned, acquired, obtained, got, held, etc.

----------

Had adalah konjugasi dari kata kerja have yang berarti memiliki/mendapat.

Had adalah bentuk past participle  (v) dari have.

------------

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan kata had dan sinonimnya.

Tom had an A for his English test.

Tom got an A for his English test.

Mr Smith had an old car.

Mr Smith owned an old car.

Mr Smith possessed an old car.

He had a key in his hand.

He held a key in his hand.

I had the painting at an auction.

I obtained the painting at an auction.

I acquired the painting at an auction.
